Simplified, plain English: The simplified method is the easy one — $5 for every square foot of office, up to 300 sq ft (so $1,500 is the most you can claim this way). No receipts needed. The actual method takes the share of your home that's your office and applies it to your real home bills — more paperwork, but sometimes a bigger deduction.
